Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: April 5, 2026

FitAI is not a medical service. The information and AI-generated content provided are for general wellness and informational purposes only and do not constitute medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any new diet or exercise program.

1. Who We Are

FitAI ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered fitness and nutrition coaching application. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@fitai.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use FitAI:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and password (securely hashed — we never store plain-text passwords).
  • Profile data: Age, sex, height, weight, goal weight, fitness goal, and activity level. This is entered voluntarily and used to personalize your experience.
  • Usage data: AI generations used, saved workout and meal plans, weight log entries, and food log entries.
  • Payment information: If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. We never store your credit card number — only a Stripe customer ID.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your AI coach, workout plans, and meal plans based on your profile.
  • To save and sync your plans, weight log, and food log across devices.
  • To track free-tier usage and enforce plan limits.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription through Stripe.
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To improve the app through anonymized, aggregated usage analytics.

4. AI and Third-Party Services

FitAI uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate personalized fitness and nutrition content. When you interact with the AI coach or generate plans, your messages and relevant profile data are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

We also use:

  • Supabase — for database storage and authentication. Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases.
  • Stripe — for payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant.
  • Vercel — for hosting and content delivery.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase databases with row-level security enabled — meaning each user can only access their own data. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (profile, plans, weight log, food log) is permanently deleted within 30 days. You can request account deletion at any time by emailing privacy@fitai.app.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fitai.app.

8. Children's Privacy

FitAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of FitAI after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
